Solutions to Combat Climate Change

Here you can find information on how we can combat climate change.







What Governments Can Do

Governments can invest in renewable energy such as solar and wind power instead of fossil fuels. They can also create laws and policies to reduce pollution, protect forests, and encourage public transportation. Large-scale decisions made by governments can reduce emissions for entire countries.

What the World (Countries & Companies) Can Do

Countries and big companies can work together to limit global carbon emissions and develop cleaner technologies. International cooperation, climate agreements, and eco-friendly industries help create long-term global change and protect the planet for future generations.



What you can do




Turn off lights, fans, and devices when not in use. Using less electricity reduces pollution from power plants.

Carry reusable bottles, bags, and containers instead of single-use plastic. This lowers waste and pollution.

Walking, cycling, or using buses reduces the number of cars on the road and lowers carbon emissions.

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and provide oxygen. Planting even one tree helps the environment.

Do not waste water while brushing or washing. Saving water also saves the energy used to pump and treat it.

Separate waste into recycling bins and reuse materials when possible. This reduces landfill waste and saves resources.

Talk to friends and family about climate change and encourage eco-friendly habits. Awareness creates larger community impact.

Buy products that are reusable, energy-efficient, or made from sustainable materials. Small buying choices can support greener companies.
