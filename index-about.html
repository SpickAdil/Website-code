About This Website

This website was created as a Grade 9 Science Fair project to raise awareness about Climate Change and the importance of taking action to protect our planet. It explains the causes, effects, and solutions of climate change in a simple and clear way. The project is based on Sustainable Development Goal 13: Climate Action, which encourages people around the world to reduce pollution and care for the environment. Small actions by individuals can make a big difference for the future of Earth.
