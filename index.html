Climate action

What is Climate Change?

Climate change means the Earth is getting hotter because of human activities like using cars, factories, and electricity.

Why Should We Care?

Climate change causes extreme heat, floods, droughts, and rising sea levels. It affects people, animals, and the environment around the world, including the UAE.

Climate Action Starts With Us

Students can help by saving electricity, reducing plastic use, planting trees, and spreading awareness. Small actions can make a big difference.
